What are the six aims for yoga?

So let us let us look at six different aims or approaches to yoga so we can better understanding where we are going & what we are trying to acheive: sṛṣṭi (growth), śikṣaṇa (learning), rakṣaṇa (maintenance), cikitsā (therapy), ādhiātmika (spirituality) & śakti (powers).

What is the impotance of yoga in future?

Yoga is not a religion; it is a way of living that aims towards 'a healthy mind in a healthy body'. Man is a physical, mental and spiritual being; yoga helps promote a balanced development of all the three. Other forms of physical exercises, like aerobics, assure only physical well-being.

What are the 8 elements of the yoga?

The eight limbs of yoga are yama (abstinences), niyama (observances), asana (yoga postures), pranayama (breath control), pratyahara (withdrawal of the senses), dharana (concentration), dhyana (meditation) and samadhi (absorption)".
